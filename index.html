Top three activities to do at Addis Ababa

Visit National Museum Ethiopia

National Museum Ethiopia holds local archaeological finds such as the fossilized remain of early humanoids.

Go for a walk in Meskel square

Meskel Square is a square in the city of Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. It is often the site for public gathering or for demonstrations and festivals, notably, the Meskel Festival from which it takes its name.

Visit historical church

St. George's Cathedral is an Ethiopian Orthodox cathedral in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. It is noted for its distinctive octagonal form.
